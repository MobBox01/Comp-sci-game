# Void Game

[Concept]

You, a Ferret who resides in the world of vologod train to become a soldier however something occurs...

You are now chosen as a hero to defeat the void entity coming from the underworld to destroy earth

2/8 portals have been summoned and seperate heros are already going to fight them and destroy the portals you are also one of these heros who has a designated portal to destroy. 

With your power of light you can achieve this!


[Logic]
Movement -> Arrow keys
Select choice -> Enter
Rooms are all tiles you CAN speed run this game

[Boss]
Your AP computer science is not pleased, you have not done your AP CSA HW at all this year he wants you dead, he is the protector of the portal you must defeat him!

[Basic]
Basic Rooms -> Easier to walk through, probably fastest part of the game
Basic Enemies -> Easy to fight and rank up from. Simple introduction to game fighting

[Advanced]
Advanced Rooms -> More time consuming to walk through
Advanced Enemies -> Difficult to fight but reward, be wise dont try to speed run them. More frequent in advanced rooms
